ADMISSION TO UMT

Universiti Malaysia Terengganu (UMT) is a public university in Malaysia, located in Terengganu, on the east coast of Peninsular Malaysia. It was originally established in 1979 as the Centre for Fisheries and Marine Science, and later became a full-fledged university. UMT is specialized in oceanography, marine science, fisheries, and related environmental sciences, but it also offers programs in engineering, management, education, and biotechnology. Its focus is on research and education related to the ocean and coastal areas, making it one of the leading institutions in Malaysia for marine studies.
